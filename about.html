Who are we?

Lighttown Speakers is an official club of Toastmasters International. There are over 15,000 clubs in 140 different countries with +- 350,000 members.
In our welcoming and friendly setting, members are encouraged to challenge themselves, step outside of their comfort zones and unleash their full potential. Together, we celebrate each other's successes, embrace our imperfections, and champion one another on the path to becoming effective communicators and leaders.

Join us, and discover the joy of self-expression, the thrill of personal growth, and the power of community as we journey together towards excellence in communication and leadership!

What is Toastmasters International?

Toastmasters International is a non-profit organization established in 1924. It has been dedicated to helping members improve their leadership and public speaking skills for nearly a century. Members engage in personalized learning experiences which are tailored to both their professional and personal needs while getting support and constructive feedback from the other members of the club.

At Toastmasters, each member progresses through speaking and leadership tasks at their own pace, receiving valuable and tailored feedback along the way. This feedback encourages continuous improvement, prompting members to ask themselves what they can do differently or better. This learning approach is fundamental to all Toastmasters clubs worldwide, including ours!
