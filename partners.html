Support Organization

Holland Expat Center South

The Holland Expat Center South provides essential support for expatriates moving to the southern Netherlands. Offering assistance with administrative tasks, housing, taxes, and community integration, they ensure a smooth transition for expats and their families. Through collaboration with local partners, the center fosters a welcoming environment in the region, facilitating the integration of newcomers.

Community Engagement Platform

TEDx Eindhoven

TEDx Eindhoven, part of the global TEDx movement, is a key hub for innovation and community engagement in the Netherlands. Through its events spanning various topics, it sparks curiosity and facilitates discussions among attendees. By empowering participants to create new connections and ideas, TEDx Eindhoven positively impacts the cultural landscape of the city and serves as a source of inspiration for positive change, not only locally but also beyond Eindhoven's borders.
